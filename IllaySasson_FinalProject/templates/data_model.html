In my project I used 3 different datasets - Internet Users in 2018, Suicide Rates in 2016 and The 2016 World Happiness Report. Using all three datasets, I can investigate the impact of the internet on our overall happiness. Does the internet make us happy by allowing us to connect to different people and catch up to the latest news all around the world, or does it hurt us by distracting us from real life and allowing things like cyberbullying and online predators?


Internet Users
Includes information about the amount of internet users in a country. This information includes total amount of internet users, percentage of the population with access to internet and ranking of the country based on total internet users.

The World Happiness Report
Includes information about a country's happiness score in the year 2016. The happiness score is built from different variables, that were collected from a survey that was answered around the world, with different questions about the person's happiness in their current country.
Dataset Link
Suicide Rates
Includes amount of people that committed suicide out of 100k people in the year 2016, for males and females separately. The data is taken directly from the World Health Organization, which makes it very reliable.
