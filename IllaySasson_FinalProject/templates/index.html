Dangers of the Internet

The internet can be really dangerous. Some people see it as a way to bully, con or abuse others. Cybercrime is also a common feature of the internet. While the internet may seem innocent on the outside, there are places like the darkent, websites that are unsupervised and unmonitored which is always a recipe for disaster. Internet safety is one the most important things to keep in mind while browsing the internet, which is why its being taught around the world all the time.
