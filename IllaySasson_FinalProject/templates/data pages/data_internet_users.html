Internet Users


Internet Users is a dataset with information on the number of internet users in countries all around the world. This dataset is crucial to the question "How does the internet affect overall happiness?" because it allows us to see how much a country uses the internet. The dataset has a variety of different information like population, total internet users, and more. While the dataset was made with data from 2018, it is still relevant to my research - 2 years is not a lot of time, and the numbers are relatively the same. The country with the highest percentage of internet users is the Falkland Islands, probably because the population is really low and almost everyone there has phones. The country with the lowest percentage of internet users is Eritrea. Israel is ranked in the 46th place in terms of internet users percentage. The dataset was taken from the website kaggle which houses a lot of different interesting datasets.
