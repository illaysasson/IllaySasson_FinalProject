Suicide Rates


Suicide Rates in 2016 is a dataset that includes information about the number of people that committed suicide in the year 2016. This dataset helps understand the impact of the internet on people's happiness, and whether or not the internet contributes to suicides around the world. The suicides are calculated per 100k people - meaning instead of the number of people that committed suicide in total in the year 2016, it shows how much people out of 100,000 have committed suicide. In the year 2016, Russia had the largest amount of male suicides out of 100k people - 48.3 suicides in total. In contrast, Morocco had 2.5 suicides out of 100k people in 2016, which is the smallest amount out of every country. Israel had 8.2 male suicides and 2.4 female suicides in 2016. The data was taken directly from WHO - The World Health Organization.
